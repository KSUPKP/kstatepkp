Our Executive Council 

​We strive for in-house members to take part in our executive council. Normally juniors and seniors our “EC” is able to participate in Pi Kapp College, a certified leadership development weekend spent in Dallas, Texas over winter break. Younger members of the chapter have the opportunity to attend Emerging Leaders training which happens in Tampa, Florida.
